⚑ Peak hour

05:00 is the single busiest hour — 44 calls, about 2.4× the average hour. First-shift startup and gas-flush calibration cluster right at shift change.

⚑ Monday is oddly quiet

Monday logs only 21 calls — less than a quarter of Thursday (85), despite sitting right between Sunday (51) and Tuesday (80). Worth a look at whether Monday coverage/reporting is thinner, not that Monday's machines are healthier.
