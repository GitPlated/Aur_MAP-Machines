05:00 is the single busiest hour — 55 calls, about 2.8× the average hour. First-shift startup and gas-flush calibration cluster right at shift change.

Monday logs only 25 calls — less than a third of Sunday (77) or Tuesday (81), despite sitting right between them. Worth a look at whether Monday coverage/reporting is thinner, not that Monday's machines are healthier.

Mechanical-failure language — "seal" (36), "sensor" (33), "belt" (24), "blade" (13), "knife" (1) — totals 107 mentions, more than "flush" + "gas" combined (75). Widening the scope past oxygen changes the story: this is a mechanical-wear problem as much as a calibration one.
