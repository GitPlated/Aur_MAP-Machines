⚑ Mechanical beats oxygen still

Mechanical-failure language — "seal" (36), "sensor" (22), "belt" (23), "blade" (10), "knife" (1) — totals 92 mentions, more than "flush" + "gas" combined (73). Widening the scope past oxygen still changes the story: this is a mechanical-wear problem as much as a calibration one.

⚑ Biggest lever
