05:00 and 07:00 are tied as the busiest hours — 39 calls each, about 2.2× the average hour. First-shift startup and gas-flush calibration cluster right at shift change.

Monday logs only 34 calls — well under half of Thursday (86), despite sitting right between Sunday (51) and Tuesday (57). Worth a look at whether Monday coverage/reporting is thinner, not that Monday's machines are healthier.

⚑ Mechanical beats oxygen still

Mechanical-failure language — "seal" (37), "sensor" (27), "belt" (24), "blade" (13), "knife" (1) — totals 102 mentions, more than "flush" + "gas" combined (77). Widening the scope past oxygen still changes the story: this is a mechanical-wear problem as much as a calibration one.

⚑ Biggest lever

76% of call-outs (319 of 420) closed with no root cause logged at all — the single biggest opportunity to actually cut repeat calls.
